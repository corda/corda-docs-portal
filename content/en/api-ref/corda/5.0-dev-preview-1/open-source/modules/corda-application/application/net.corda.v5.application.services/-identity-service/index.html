IdentityService

@DoNotImplement interface IdentityService : CordaServiceInjectable, CordaFlowInjectable

An identity service maintains a directory of parties by their associated distinguished name/public keys, and thus supports the lookup of a party if given its key or its X.500 name.

The service supports two types of identities:

  • A well-known identity, represented by a Party structure, which is a public identity currently or previously registered under the specific X.500 name in the membership group. See MemberLookupService for retrieving actual and historic well-known identities from the membership group.
  • A confidential identity, represented by AnonymousParty structure, which is only distributed on a "need-to-know" basis (typically between parties in a transaction being built). The service links confidential identity to a particular X.500 name.
